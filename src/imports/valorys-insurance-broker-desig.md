GLOBAL DESIGN RULES:

- All section transitions: alternating white / #F4F6F9 gray
- Large diagonal ghost shapes (#1B2A4A at 3% opacity) as background
texture in gray sections (like subtle watermark)
- Card border-radius: 12px
- Button border-radius: 50px (pill shape)
- Hover states: cards lift with shadow, buttons darken 10%,
nav links show red underline
- Floating phone button bottom-right: red circle, white phone icon
- All images: rounded corners 12px, slight warm overlay
- Mobile: hamburger menu, stacked cards, full-width buttons
- Fonts from Google Fonts:
Display/headings: "Playfair Display" or "DM Serif Display"
Body: "DM Sans" or "Outfit"
